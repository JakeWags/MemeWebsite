Rules/Explanation

This game is essentially the "Call Out" phase of Craps.

The way it works is you roll two dice and add up the rolls. Simple!

On the very first roll, a 7 or an 11 results in the player winning, whereas a 2, 3, or 12 results in a loss.

If none of these are rolled, the number rolled is set as the "point."

The player rolls again. If the numbers rolled don't add up to the "point" or 7, they roll again.

If the "point" value is rolled, the player wins. If 7 is rolled, the house wins.

Have fun!
