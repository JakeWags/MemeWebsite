How to play:

The rules of the game are simple.
The first step is to select a number you will be betting on.
Then, you click roll. The computer will roll three dice.

If one is equal to your chosen number, you get your bet, if two, you get twice your bet, and if three, your bet is tripled.
However, if none are equal, you lose your bet.
If you run out of money, click reset.

Have fun!
